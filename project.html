Caen

The town has a memorial to the Normandy landings, and there is a castle in the middle of town. It takes about two and a half hours to get to Paris. In summer, it is not humid and the temperature is comfortable at about 30 degrees Celsuis, but in winter, it is very cold due to the proximity of the sea. It does not snow much.
