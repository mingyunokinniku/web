France (République française)

Previous Next

It is located in the west of Europe, bordering countries such as Germany, Spain, Belguim, and Switzerland. The capital is Paris, and the area of France is about 550,000 km². About 65 million people live in France. In France, there is such a wide variety of cheeses that it is said that each village has its own characteristics.

Also, July 14th is the day to celebrate the French Revolution, and the tricolor flag is flown all over the city. Then, fireworks are set off in various places to celebrate the occasion in a big way. In Paris, there will be military parade, the president will give a speech, and there will be music festivals and dance parties all over the country.
